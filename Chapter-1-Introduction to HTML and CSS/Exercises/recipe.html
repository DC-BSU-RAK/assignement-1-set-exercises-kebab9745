Creamy Tomato Pasta

Easy and yummy pasta!

Stuff you need:

  • Pasta
  • Tomato sauce
  • Cream
  • Cheese

How to make it:

  1. Cook pasta.
  2. Mix sauce and cream.
  3. Put on pasta.
  4. Add cheese.
